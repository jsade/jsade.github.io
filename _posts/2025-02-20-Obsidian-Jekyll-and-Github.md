I'm using [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md) daily for my note taking, meeting minutes, diary entries, and task management. And this list seems to be growing each month.

Sometimes my writing includes content or topics that I think might be interesting to others. So, why not make it easy to publish these notes straight from Obsidian to some public web server.

After studying what options there are, I've settled into [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/) and [Github Pages](https://pages.github.com/).
